I'm Mohamad, a software developer and researcher based in Vienna. I came to Austria from Syria in 2015 and have been here for eleven years.

I've been building Cortex — an AI-powered journaling app that helps people make sense of their health over time. I built it alone because I needed something like it and it didn't exist.

Along the way, I've learned a lot about navigating systems that weren't designed for people like me — and about what it takes to keep building when the path isn't clear.

This is where I share what I've experienced and what I'm working on.
